About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology stands as one of India's premier engineering institutions, fostering innovation and technological excellence since 1984. With a sprawling campus that houses state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, VIT has become a breeding ground for the next generation of engineers, scientists, and innovators.

The university's commitment to hands-on learning and interdisciplinary collaboration makes it the perfect home for cutting-edge robotics research and development.

Our Mission

RoboVITics is VIT's flagship robotics club — a forge where raw engineering talent is hammered into competition-grade machines. We don't just study robotics; we build them, break them, and build them stronger.

What We Do

Three specialized divisions push the boundaries: Orcus builds arena combat bots, Artemis engineers legged locomotion platforms, and Avatar develops humanoid dexterous systems.

Build. Battle. Innovate.

From midnight soldering sessions to international arenas, we live at the intersection of engineering precision and creative chaos. Every bolt tightened, every line of code pushed — it all leads to machines that move, think, and fight.

ORCUS — Combat Division

The arena is our proving ground. Orcus engineers design and fabricate heavyweight combat robots equipped with devastating kinetic energy weapons — from drum spinners that shatter titanium to pneumatic flippers that launch opponents airborne.

  • Kinetic drum spinners up to 15,000 RPM
  • Pneumatic flipper systems at 200 PSI
  • AR500 steel armor plating
  • Custom ESC and failsafe systems

ARTEMIS — Legged Robotics Division

Wheels are limiting. Artemis builds multi-legged autonomous platforms capable of traversing rough terrain, climbing stairs, and maintaining balance on unstable surfaces.

  • 12-DOF quadruped platforms
  • Real-time inverse kinematics engine
  • LiDAR + depth camera SLAM navigation
  • Custom servo brackets & linkages
